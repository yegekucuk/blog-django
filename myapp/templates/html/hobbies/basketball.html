The Essence of Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played on a rectangular court, where two teams compete to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop. With its roots deeply embedded in American culture, basketball has evolved into a global phenomenon, captivating audiences with its blend of athleticism, skill, and strategy.
